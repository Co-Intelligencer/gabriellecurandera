About Gabrielle

I am a therapist and facilitator of ancestral medicines — Kambo, Bufo, Rapé and Sananga, among others. I guide women's circles, temazcal sweat lodges and cacao circles; I am a fire keeper, and I offer in-person and online workshops on self-knowledge and spirituality, as well as retreats that open consciousness.

My calling is the transmission of ancestral knowledge and the accompaniment of personal and spiritual development. My specialty is group facilitation and leadership — holding many hearts in one circle. I am in constant change: I love learning everything that serves the awakening of consciousness and planetary service.

I have also served as a volunteer and therapist in environmental conservation, animal rescue, community aid and children's workshops — and I share the Saint Germain meditations with my community, online and in person.
